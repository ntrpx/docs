## Community Guidelines

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Be Respectful" icon="heart">
We're all here to learn and improve. Treat others the way you'd want to be treated. No personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Stay Evidence-Based" icon="microscope">
Anecdotes are welcome, but distinguish them from proven science. Cite sources when making claims. We value rigor over hype.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="No Medical Advice" icon="user-doctor">
We discuss optimization, not treatment. Don't diagnose, prescribe, or recommend discontinuing medications. Always consult healthcare professionals.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Keep It Legal" icon="scale-balanced">
No discussion of controlled substances, illegal sourcing, or activities that violate laws in your jurisdiction.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
