## Overview

Lion's Mane (Hericium erinaceus) is a nootropic mushroom containing erinacines and hericenones that stimulate Nerve Growth Factor (NGF) synthesis, supporting neuroplasticity and cognitive function.

## Taxonomy Position

- **Domain:** Adaptogens & Herbal Modulators
- **Category:** Nootropic Herbs & Mushrooms (4.2)
- **Subcategory:** NGF Synthesis
