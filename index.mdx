## Method

<Note>
We don't chase trends or proprietary blends. Every formula starts with biology, validates with evidence, and optimizes for real outcomes.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Map the Biology" icon="dna">
    Identify the biological systems that govern cognitive performance: neurotransmitter pathways, circadian rhythms, cellular energetics, and stress response axes. Understand *what* needs optimization before reaching for compounds.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Analyse Ingredients" icon="microscope">
    Evaluate each compound through the lens of mechanism, bioavailability, safety, and synergy. No ingredient enters a formula without clear evidence and a defined role. Our [Ingredient Taxonomy](/manuals/ingedient-manual/taxonomy) classifies 200+ compounds by biological mechanism.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Formulate Systems" icon="cubes-stacked">
    Combine compounds into coherent systems that work *with* your biology across the circadian cycle. Optimize for synergy, minimize redundancy, and avoid antagonistic interactions. The result: formulas that perform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
